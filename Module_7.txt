Log:
A record of event that occur within an organization's systems and network.

Common log sources:-

->Firewall Log: A firewall log is a record of attempted or established connections for incoming traffic from the internet.
It also includes outbound requests to the internet from within the network.

->Network Log: A network log is a record of all computers and devices that enter and leave the network. It also records 
connections between devices and services on the network.

->Server Log: A server log is a record of events related to services, such as websites, emails, or file shares. 
It includes actions such as login, password, and username requests.
